# MusiQLx

## Introduction

MusiQLx is a music collection manager designed with user experience as the top priority. My aim is to provide a self-hosted solution with an experience comparable to commercial solutions such as Spotify and Apple Music, offering seamless organization and management of your music library. MusiQLx caters to users who prefer sourcing their music from platforms like YouTube Music, Deezer, and MusicBrainz, providing a diverse array of options while ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience.

Yes, this is a Lidarr alternative. One of the main goals is to make the API compatible with Lidarr so it will be a drop-in replacement for third-party applications.

## Key Features

- [ ] **Diverse Source Options**: MusiQLx utilizes YouTube Music, Deezer, and MusicBrainz as sources for metadata and music files, offering a wider range of options for users.
- [ ] **Automatic Track Detection**: Just like Lidarr, MusiQLx automatically detects new tracks from your favorite artists, making it convenient to stay updated with the latest releases.
- [ ] **Automatic Suggestions**: MusiQLx offers automatic suggestions for new tracks based on your listening history and preferences, enhancing your music discovery experience.
- [ ] **Auto Download Suggestions**: Users have the option to enable automatic downloading of suggested tracks, streamlining the process of expanding your music library with recommended content.
- [ ] **Library Scanning and Management**: Scan your existing library and download any missing tracks, ensuring your collection is complete and up-to-date.
- [ ] **Robust Error Handling**: MusiQLx features automatic failed download handling, attempting alternative releases if one fails, ensuring a smoother downloading experience.
- [ ] **Manual Search**: Users can manually search for specific releases or investigate why a release was not downloaded automatically, offering greater control and insight.
- [ ] **Flexible Track Renaming**: Customize how your tracks are renamed with MusiQLx's fully configurable track renaming options, ensuring consistency and organization in your library.
- [ ] **Integration Support**: MusiQLx seamlessly integrates with media centers like Emby and Plex for notification, library updates, and metadata management.
- [ ] **Support for Specials and Multi-Album Releases**: MusiQLx fully supports specials and multi-album releases, ensuring all aspects of your music collection are covered.
- [ ] **User-Friendly Interface**: Enjoy a beautiful UI that makes navigating and managing your music collection a breeze.
